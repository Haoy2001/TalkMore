Understanding TalkMore

What you can do and what this site is about, a brief synopsis...

Are you feeling happy, sad, mad, talkative, joyful, need to talk to someone? On this site you can take part in any sort of conversation.

Here you are able to connect with other people who share the same interest in our "Find my Twin" tab. Are there things you want to argue against or for? In our "Lets Argue!" option you can share option your personal opinions, point of view and facts you know. If you are really here because you are bored or want to make some new friends and find out about people you can always access our "Random" option and chat with people all around the globe without any known related connections, just a random friend you will make :).

More about our "Lets Argue!" option

On a more in-depth version of this great option is if you feel you need to get a point across or you need to share your point of view on any sort of current debatable or proven issue or current world event you can improve your defendant/lawyer skills or just becoming a better arguer. Even if you need to talk about your own point of view on an a law that shouldn't be legal or something that should be legalized.

How this works you ask?

Well simple, you will choose a "For" or "Against" tag and select what you want to argue against or for under our many topics. If none of these interest you, you will write your own topic and see who else wants to argue with it. If you don't know what you want to argue about you can go under the "topic requests" and chose a topic that someone has selected and written themselves and discuss it with them.

More about our "Find My twin" option

If you want to know all fo the features and use this option to its max potential, in this section we have you covered! In this option you can talk to people all around the globe that share your likings. under the "interests" tag you will write and choose as many as you desire or just make your own and talk to people interested in your chosen topic(s). If you want to find a random interest someone has made themselves just simply go under "interest request" and find someone with a great or odd interest and make some friends.
